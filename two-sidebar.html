Chevrolet Malibu

Highs Attractive design, comfortable ride, playful chassis.

The designers who worked on the new generation of Chevrolet Malibu tried to make the exterior of the model more aggressive. The new model has a completely new appearance, unlike its previous generations. Thanks to the curved and flexible lines, the appearance of the sedan is more dynamic and has a very pleasant shape. Malibu's signature dual radiator grille, which is easily recognizable, is now equipped with a narrower shape of the main LED optics. The embossed trim on the sides and the rear of the body are largely similar to the Impala sedan model, and in general, most of the body elements are similar to the legendary Camaro model, but this is only an external similarity. Due to the careful development of some details of the body, we managed to achieve excellent aerodynamic performance. In the market of Uzbekistan, the car is offered in 4 different colors (pearl white, white-gray, dark gray and black colors).

Design

High-quality materials are used in the design of the interior, due to this, the salon now looks much better than the interior of the models of previous generations. A comfortable minimum set of buttons on the center console, the sedan got a new instrument panel and steering wheel. In the center of the central console is a 7.0-inch touch screen of the MyLink entertainment-information complex. The driver sits on a spacious seat with improved back and side support. The steering wheel is more forward, and the on-board computer with analog sensors is located behind it. Rear passengers have an extra 33 mm of volume and can enjoy an all-round comfortable ride with noise isolation.

In terms of safety, buyers of the new generation of Malibu will not be disappointed. It is equipped with 8 airbags, a telescopic steering column, the safety system also includes automatic hill braking, automatic cruise control, traction control (anti-skid control) and parking assist with front and rear sensors. includes functions such as system.
